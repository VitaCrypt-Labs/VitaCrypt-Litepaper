## About VitaCrypt

The only consumer app creating a unified health personal profile by securely integrating real-time environmental data, genetics, wearables, and lifestyle — powered by **Fully Homomorphic Encryption (FHE)** and **Blind Computing**.

Your health data stays encrypted, even during computation. Personalization happens on ciphertext. No human — including us — sees the raw data.

## Why FHE

Fully Homomorphic Encryption allows computation on encrypted data without ever decrypting it. This means we can run AI analysis, cross-reference against 30M+ peer-reviewed studies (PubMed, Cochrane, Scopus), and generate personalized recommendations — while your raw biological data never leaves encrypted form.
